Cookie Policy

Last updated: June 2026

This Cookie Policy explains what cookies are, how SimPhantom uses them, and the choices you have. By continuing to use our platform, you consent to our use of cookies as described here.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device by your web browser when you visit a website. They allow the site to remember information about your visit like staying logged in between page loads so you don't have to re-enter it each time.

Cookies We Use

We keep our cookie use minimal. Here is every cookie we set:

Cookie Purpose Duration
sessionid Keeps you logged in across pages Session / 2 weeks
csrftoken Security prevents forgery attacks 1 year
darkmode Remembers theme preference 1 year

What We Do NOT Use

✗

We do not use tracking cookies or third-party ad networks.

✗

We do not use Google Analytics or similar cross-site tracking services.

✗

We do not share cookie data with advertisers.

Essential Cookies

The sessionid and csrftoken cookies are essential for the platform to function. Without them, you cannot log in or make secure requests. These cannot be disabled without breaking core functionality.

Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings:

Chrome Settings → Privacy and Security → Cookies
Firefox Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies
Safari Preferences → Privacy → Cookies
Edge Settings → Privacy → Cookies and site permissions

Note: disabling the essential session cookie will prevent you from logging into SimPhantom.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever add new cookies for example, if we add analytics in the future we will update this policy and notify you through the platform before those cookies are set.

Questions about cookies?

Contact our support team for any clarifications.
