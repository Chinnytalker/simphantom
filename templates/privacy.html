Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026  ·  Effective immediately

SimPhantom ("we", "us", or "our") operates the SimPhantom platform at this website. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our services. By using SimPhantom, you agree to the practices described here.

1

Information We Collect

Account Information

When you register, we collect your username, email address, and a hashed password. We never store your plaintext password.

Transaction Data

We record wallet top-ups, orders placed, and service usage to maintain accurate billing records and order history.

Payment Information

Payments are processed by Paystack. We never see or store your card number, bank account details, or PIN. Only a transaction reference and status are stored on our side.

Usage Data

We may log IP addresses, browser type, and pages visited for security monitoring and fraud prevention. These logs are retained for 90 days.

Support Communications

Messages you send through our support ticket system are stored so our team can resolve your issue. You may request deletion of closed tickets.

2

How We Use Your Information

  • →To create and manage your account
  • →To process payments and deliver services you purchase
  • →To send transactional emails (order confirmations, wallet alerts)
  • →To respond to support requests
  • →To detect and prevent fraud, abuse, or unauthorised access
  • →To improve our platform based on aggregate usage patterns

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

4

Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. If you request account deletion, we will delete your personal information within 30 days, except where we are required by law to retain certain records (e.g., financial transaction records for 5 years under Nigerian law).

5

Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including encrypted connections (HTTPS), hashed passwords, and access controls. However, no system is 100% secure. If you suspect your account has been compromised, contact us immediately.

6

Your Rights

Under the Nigeria Data Protection Act (NDPA) 2023 and applicable regulations, you have the right to:

  • ✓Access the personal data we hold about you
  • ✓Correct inaccurate information
  • ✓Request deletion of your data
  • ✓Object to processing of your data
  • ✓Data portability (export your data)

To exercise any of these rights, contact our support team.

7

Cookies

We use essential cookies to keep you logged in and maintain session state. See our Cookie Policy for full details.

8

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes by posting a notice on the platform. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.
