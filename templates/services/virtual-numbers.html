What is a virtual phone number?

A virtual phone number is a temporary number that can receive SMS messages and OTP codes without needing a physical SIM card. SimPhantom provides numbers from 190+ countries, available instantly.

What happens if I don't receive an OTP?

If no OTP is received within 5 minutes, SimPhantom automatically cancels the number on our provider and refunds the full amount to your wallet. You can reorder immediately with no extra steps.

Which apps can I verify with a virtual number?

You can verify accounts on WhatsApp, Telegram, Instagram, TikTok, Facebook, Twitter/X, Google, Snapchat, and hundreds of other apps and websites that require phone number verification.

How do I pay for virtual numbers in Nigeria?

SimPhantom uses Paystack for payments, supporting bank transfers, debit/credit cards, and USSD. You top up your wallet balance in Naira, then spend it per number. Minimum top-up is just ₦500.

Can I use the same virtual number multiple times?

Virtual numbers are single-use one number per verification session. This ensures your privacy and prevents accounts from being linked. If you need to verify multiple accounts, simply buy a new number each time.

Why do USA WhatsApp numbers sometimes not deliver an OTP?

WhatsApp actively blocks many US virtual number ranges to prevent abuse. If a US number fails, your money is automatically refunded and you can reorder. For higher success rates on WhatsApp, try numbers from countries like Lithuania, Kazakhstan, or Indonesia they tend to work more reliably.
